Spice Level Guide

Find your comfort zone on our traditional heat index

Mild
🌶️

Gentle warmth with heavy focus on flavor and aroma. Suitable for everyone.

Medium
🌶️🌶️

A pleasant tickle of heat. Typical of classic South Indian table powders.

Hot
🌶️🌶️🌶️

Authentic home-style heat. Gives the signature kick without overwhelming.

Extra Hot
🌶️🌶️🌶️🌶️

Our Philosophy

From Headmaster's Desk to Kitchen Counter

For 30+ years, Amma ran her school with warmth and an iron hand. Today, she applies those same standards to every jar.

📐

Headmaster Standard

No shortcuts. Every raw mango chunk is cut to a precise geometry, and every podi roasted under Amma's direct supervision.

🥘

Small Batch Kitchen

We cook in tiny micro-batches. Slow-roasting ingredients preserves native oils and natural textures that commercial machinery destroys.

🧠

Andhra Taste Memory

Recreating the taste profile of summer holidays in the villages — robust salt, cold-pressed sesame oil, and Guntur chillies.

👩‍🏫

From Amma's Kitchen

From roasting spices at dawn to hand-tying jar seals, this kitchen represents Amma's lifelong commitment to doing things perfectly.
